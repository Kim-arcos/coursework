Bears

Bears have large bodies with thick legs, long snouts, small ears, hefty paws and short tails.

  1. Ollie
  2. Mona

Giraffes

Giraffes are the tallest land mammals with necks as long as 6 feet, and have a uniqye brown and white coat.

  1. Frankie
  2. Coconut

Lions

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down and killing prey.

  1. Mella
  2. Karl

Monkeys

Monkeys are small and medium sized primates with long tails and they use all 4 of their limbs for climbing trees.

  1. Cookie
  2. Earl
  3. Banana Pudding

Alligators

Alligators are large cold blooded reptiles with short legs, a long tail, tough skin, sharp teeth and a wide snout. They can grow up to 15 feet long and weigh about 1,000 pounds!

  1. Wren
  2. Aspen
  3. Mika
